The problem

No founder wakes up
wanting compliance.

They wake up wondering whether they forgot something. Whether SARS will penalise them. Whether they still qualify for funding, or whether a tender will be thrown out over one missed requirement. What they're actually buying isn't compliance — it's certainty.

And certainty is hard to come by because the landscape is scattered across CIPC, SARS, the Department of Labour, the Financial Intelligence Centre, the National Credit Regulator, POPIA, municipalities and a long tail of sector bodies — each with its own processes, language and deadlines, none designed to talk to the others.

A dozen institutions. One founder. No map.

The vision

One place that always has the answer.

Not a consultant you call twice a year — the platform you return to the moment a compliance question appears. Log in, and three things are immediately clear.

What applies to you

Every regulation that touches your specific business — filtered by industry, size, turnover and whether you hold staff or customer data.

What to do next

The exact action required, why it matters, and when it's due — turned from a wall of legislation into a clear, ordered list.

Who can help

When a task needs a human, a verified specialist is one tap away — labour, tax, POPIA, financial compliance and more.

Deliver that clarity consistently and Sentinel stops being a service provider. It becomes part of the infrastructure businesses run on — the same way they rely on their bank or their accounting software.

The ascent

Five stages. Each one earns the next.

We don't leap to the end state. We climb — building the knowledge, trust and data that make each stage possible before we take it.

  1. STAGE 01Learn

    Consulting & Advisory

    We sit with founders and small businesses and learn exactly where compliance breaks down — which questions recur, which answers are hardest to find, and what people will genuinely pay to solve. Every consultation is insight, not just revenue.

  2. STAGE 02Map

    Digital Compliance Platform

    A handful of structured questions — industry, staff, turnover, whether you hold customer data — return a personalised roadmap of what applies and what to do. At this stage the platform replaces confusion, not expertise.

  3. STAGE 03Watch

    Compliance Management

    Compliance isn't an event. Annual returns, tax deadlines, labour filings, licence renewals, shifting regulations — Sentinel monitors them continuously and tells you before they're due. The promise shifts from becoming compliant to staying that way.

  4. STAGE 04Assist

    AI Compliance Assistant

    Only once the expertise, relationships and data exist do we bring AI in earnest. Trained on thousands of real South African cases rather than generic theory, it answers questions, drafts documents and guides — an edge competitors can't shortcut.

  5. STAGE 05Connect

    Marketplace & Ecosystem

    Rather than deliver every service ourselves, we connect businesses to verified specialists — labour, POPIA, tax, quality assurance. We stop competing with professionals and start creating work for them, as the platform that facilitates it all.

Each stage funds and teaches the next. Consulting reveals the real problem. The platform earns adoption. Adoption produces data. Data makes the AI genuinely useful. And the trust built across all four is what lets a marketplace stand.
